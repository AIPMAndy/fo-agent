## What is this?

`fo-agent` is a **pure frontend, zero-dependency, browser-openable prototype for a self-awareness AI agent**.

It does not try to plan your whole life or flood you with “correct answers.” Its scope is intentionally smaller:

> When you feel anxious, stuck, emotionally entangled, angry, or mentally drained, it helps you see what is actually trapping you — and what one small action you can take today.

That is also what makes it different from many “general AI assistant” products:

**Fo is not trying to be more powerful. It is trying to be calmer, lighter, and more grounding.**

---

## What problem does it solve?

A lot of AI products optimize for being:
- smarter
- broader
- more human-like
- more capable

But in many real moments, what people need is not a more powerful agent. They need something that helps them:

- reduce inner friction
- create distance from emotion
- step back from spiraling thoughts
- shrink a life-sized problem into one doable action today

That is the use case `fo-agent` is built for.

## What it does today

You type a question or emotional state such as:

- I feel anxious and keep worrying I will make the wrong choice
- I cannot let go of a relationship
- I am stuck and cannot decide what direction to take
- I am angry and resentful

Fo returns three structured parts:

1. **what is trapping you**
2. **what you really need to see**
3. **one action for today**

Why this structure works:
- it does not spiral into endless advice
- it avoids overwhelming the user
- it fits emotional states better than long-form output
